Inferno Rules Mission-Structure-Agreements

To progress as a team toward victory in a friendly and fun community.

All players will play in official matches, and give us the best chance of winning based on their 1) cohesiveness with the team 2) availability to play. All 8 players from all teams will play plenty, as many of us have other obligations. We will have other trainees in discord that are part of the team, but not in the league roster, that will practice with the team and participate in team scrims against other teams. The trainees will be invited into the official league team of 8 when they are at a competitive level and when slots open. Practices will be based on the teams availability.

We will not put down other players on the team. We will do our best to communicate with our team (games). We will do our best to be responsible (attend practice).
